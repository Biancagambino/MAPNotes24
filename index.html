About ClosetGeniusapp

ClosetGenius is your personalized AI-powered wardrobe assistant designed to maximize your existing clothing and recommend stylish outfits for every occasion. Powered by cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning, ClosetGenius promotes sustainable fashion and reduces textile waste by helping you make the most of your wardrobe.

Download App

Features

  • AI-driven outfit recommendations based on weather, occasion, and style preferences
  • Effortlessly catalog and manage your wardrobe
  • Track clothing wear frequency and sustainability insights
  • Receive personalized alerts for your most underutilized clothing items
  • Submit looks to our shop and sell/trade with a community of users

Sustainability

At ClosetGenius, we believe in promoting sustainable fashion by encouraging users to wear their existing wardrobe and avoid unnecessary purchases. By analyzing wear patterns and making personalized recommendations, we aim to help users reduce their fashion footprint.
