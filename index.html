About ClosetGenius

ClosetGenius is your personalized AI-powered wardrobe assistant designed to maximize your existing clothing and recommend stylish outfits for every occasion. Powered by cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning, ClosetGenius promotes sustainable fashion and reduces textile waste by helping you make the most of your wardrobe.

Download App

Features

  • AI-driven outfit recommendations based on weather, occasion, and style preferences
  • Effortlessly catalog and manage your wardrobe
  • Track clothing wear frequency and sustainability insights
  • Receive personalized alerts for your most underutilized clothing items
  • Submit looks to our shop and sell/trade with a community of users

Sustainability

ClosetGenius promotes sustainability by helping users make the most of their existing wardrobe, encouraging creative outfit combinations instead of buying new clothes. By using AI to track and categorize clothing items, the app reduces the need for excessive consumption, which in turn decreases waste. This approach aligns with environmentally conscious behavior, as it aims to extend the lifecycle of garments, minimizing the fashion industry's environmental footprint. Through better wardrobe management, users are empowered to make sustainable fashion choices that contribute to a lower carbon footprint and reduced textile waste.
